About Arctic Scholar

Arctic Scholar enables researchers, educators, interested private sector entities, government agencies, and the general public to access and share arctic data and information contained in assorted formats including publications, grey literature, research licenses, photo archives, field notes, and project metadata from arctic field stations. It is a component of the ArcticConnect project, a collaboration between the Arctic Institute of North America, the University of Calgary, CANARIE, and Cybera.

This site is open source software. It is MIT Licensed and the source is available on GitHub. If you have any suggestions or find any issues, please use the issue tracker on GitHub to contact our development and maintenance team.

If you have general questions or comments about Arctic Scholar or Arctic Connect, please contact us by email at info@arcticconnect.org.
